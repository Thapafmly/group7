About the game

This game is about treasure hunting, where a player navigates to the target continents and countries followed by the airports and gathers the scores as a "treasure" along the way. First, the program prompts the user to enter his/her name, then the player selects the desired options from all the continents, countries, and one out of three airports of the corresponding country. After each navigation attempt, the player receives information on the number of remaining attempts, the total number of attempts (3), weather, and the score. The player gets the notification of weather condition of each arrival points. The weather information includes the temperature and feels like (temperature) in degree celsius, and humidity in g/m^3. The program fetches the weather data from API of https://openweathermap.org/api, maps data from the leaflet library,and data elements like continents, country, airport, municipality from flight_game database.

You would like to join Elon Musk's billionaire party, but you're very poor. BUT WAIT! Your grandfather wants to lend you his private jet. He's coming with you to collect golden treasures around the world. Your goal is to get rich by collecting points and to get into Elon's party.

Our game is easier to understand! The goal is to find treasures across the world, and the player gets to decide where! You get 3 attempts to do so. First, you choose a continent and then after that a country. Then you can choose from 3 random airports to find the treasure. If you get lucky, you find the reward and claim 500 points! After that, the game ends and you see how many points you have collected so far.

We hope, you'll have a great time playing our game, and have a good treasure-hunting time!
